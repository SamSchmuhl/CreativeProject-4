PEOPLE

- Players in the Great Game -

There are many in Erastia who would claim power. These are the players in the Great Game. From the Lords of the Night-Wode in the north, Irithiel and Ilbyran, to the rising power of Duke Pelopidas in the southern reaches of Baresh, to the new Queen of the Aedmari Clans, Rasaty Drothar, the pieces have been set.
While some fear what this new age is to bring, others see an opportunity.
Let the die be cast!
