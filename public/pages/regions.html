THE STRUGGLE FOR POWER

The Clans of Aedmari have emerged as the successors to the Gods, wielding unprecedented power. The established power in the lands of the Emerald Sea, the Grand Duchies of Baresh, have begun a desperate gambit to curb this power, via the Old Ways. Meanwhile, Elenon, the Night-Wode, and Tirothon, the Towerland, wait with bated breath...
