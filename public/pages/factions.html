VITARI

- The Sands Awakened -

The ancient peoples inhabiting the Tshansa, the Devouring Sands, called themselves the Vitari. Little is known about their civilization, but it is thought they were skilled sorcerers and craftsmen, brought low by a terrible cataclysm. Yet rumor and prophecy say that that their death may not be permanent...

THE RIFT INFERNAL

- The Fall of Heaven -

When the Gods of the Third Ascension were stripped of their power in the Siphoning, their leagues of Angelic followers were left without cause. It is said that a Rift has been opened in the Celestial Spheres, and those who dwell there are quite unlike the Great Angels of old...

LITHI

- The Wanderers of Stone -

Since the Primordial Chaos, Falaxryt-Bened, the Stonesoul, has survived, weathering the destruction of every Ascension thus far. The Lithi, loyal followers of the Stonesoul, follow in its wake, obeying its every command. Within this new vaccuum of power, it remains to be seen whether or not they will make a move...
